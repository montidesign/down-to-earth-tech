## IT Consultancy vs. IT Services: What's the Difference?





### What Is the Difference Between IT Consultancy and IT Services





Asking, "What is the difference between IT consultancy and IT services?" means we need to look into the essence of planning. IT consultancy embodies the strategic phase. Planning, guidance, and the creation of roadmaps take place there. IT professionals engage in the identified initiatives. They offer assistance with implementation and operation. The consultant's role in guiding organizations towards their digital future is shown by the following:






- **Advisory Role:** The goal of consulting is to help understand company objectives. They plan a strategic technology roadmap customized to those objectives. It involves offering knowledgeable direction for handling the digital environment.




- **Strategic Planning:** Consultants analyze the technological standing of a company. Then, they identify improvement areas and recommend solutions that align with long-term goals.




- **Execution and Operational Support:** This is where IT services step in. Then, they use the technology solutions as advised by consultants. This covers the setting up of networks, software, and hardware.




- **Management and Maintenance:** IT services ensure technology systems' ongoing efficiency and reliability. They handle updates, troubleshooting, and user support.
